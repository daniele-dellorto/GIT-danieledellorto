ABOUT

DanDee project comes from the attitude of some guys to look around and discover beauty in everyday reality. In a world that runs faster and faster and where people have no time to stop and look at each other, paying attention to what surrounds us is the first way to reappropriate our context and understand what we are.

The shots that you will find in the gallery are all made in analog and developed at home. The analogy photography most of all forces you to accurately design the shot, choose the angle and the point of view. This means that you are subjected to an extremely careful analysis of the subject. you are forced to know your reality.
